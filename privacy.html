Overview

Situationship Oracle is an entertainment app. We don’t offer medical, legal, or professional advice. We use advertising to keep the app free.

Data we collect

  • App analytics (crash logs, performance, usage metrics) to improve the app.
  • Advertising data from our ad partners to show ads and measure performance.

Advertising & tracking (iOS)

On iOS, Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) controls access to the advertising identifier (IDFA). If you allow tracking, ads may be more relevant; if you don’t, you’ll still see non-personalized ads.

Your choices

  • Change iOS tracking permission anytime: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
  • Region-specific consent (e.g., EEA/UK) may appear via additional prompts if required.
